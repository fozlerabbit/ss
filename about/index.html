About ScriptySphere

A youth-driven organization committed to building tomorrow's Bangladesh through technology, education, and social development.

Our Vision

We envision a Bangladesh where every young person has access to technology education and the opportunity to drive positive change in their communities.

Our Mission

To integrate technology education, youth leadership development, environmental awareness, and mental well-being through innovative programmes and community engagement.

Our Values

Innovation

Embracing new technologies and creative solutions to address Bangladesh's challenges.

Inclusion

Ensuring equal opportunities and access for all youth regardless of background.

Sustainability

Building programs and solutions for the long-term future of Bangladesh.

Community

Fostering collaboration, mutual support, and collective growth.

Our Journey

Foundation

January 2024

ScriptySphere was founded by a group of passionate young technologists in Dhaka with a vision to democratize technology education across Bangladesh.

First Technology Camp

March 2024

Launched our first technology camp in Dhaka, reaching over 100 students with hands-on programming workshops.

Expansion Across Divisions

June 2024

Extended our reach to all 8 divisions of Bangladesh, establishing local coordinator networks in each region.

1000+ Students Milestone

October 2024

Celebrated reaching over 1000 students through our various programmes, with 25+ successful programme completions.

Digital Platform Launch

Coming Soon

Launching our comprehensive digital learning platform to reach even more students across Bangladesh.
